Our Services

1. Bedridden Patient Care

  • 24x7 trained nursing staff for complete care of bedridden patients.
  • Assistance in feeding, bathing, oral hygiene & daily routine.
  • Prevention of bedsores with proper positioning & skin care.
  • Monitoring vital signs (BP, Pulse, Temperature, SpO₂).
  • Compassionate emotional support for patient & family.

2. Elderly Care (Geriatric Care)

  • Professional nurses & attendants for senior citizens.
  • Assistance in mobility, walking, and exercise support.
  • Regular health monitoring & timely medication.
  • Nutrition management and companionship.

3. Post-Surgical Care

  • Specialized care after operations & surgeries.
  • Wound dressing & infection prevention.
  • Pain management under doctor’s guidance.
  • Physiotherapy assistance for fast recovery.

4. Home Nursing Care

  • Skilled staff for home-based medical & personal care.
  • IV/IM injections, catheter care, Ryle’s tube feeding.
  • Diabetic care, BP & sugar monitoring.
  • Patient-specific care plan under doctor’s advice.

5. Maternity & Newborn Care

  • Trained nurses for mother & baby care.
  • Support in breastfeeding & lactation.
  • Newborn hygiene, vaccination guidance & health monitoring.
  • Postnatal exercise & recovery care for mothers.

6. Palliative & End-of-Life Care

  • Comfort-focused care for critically ill patients.
  • Pain & symptom management.
  • Emotional & spiritual support to patient and family.
  • Dignified end-of-life assistance with compassion.
